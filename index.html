About Porch Light

Porch Light is a calm, offline-first Stoic philosophy app. Each daily passage from classical public-domain Stoic texts is paired with a hand-selected, looping animated illustration that represents its core meaning.

Our free tier offers one illustrated quote per day, forever, with no hidden limits. Unlocking the Pro tier provides full sequential reading of every book, unlimited browsing, and a private local reflections journal.
